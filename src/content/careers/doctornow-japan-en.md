### Role & Achievements

**Role:** Lead of Product & Tech for Japan Expansion (Team of 3). Built & Operated User App/Web, Admin, Backend/Infra from scratch.

*   **Japan Market Entry & Pivot:** Launched Japanese App/Web services in 6 months. Flexibly expanded and redesigned systems to adapt to business model changes (Telemedicine, Medication Delivery, Commerce).
*   **Multi-Brand Architecture (MEDIMODI & Doctornow):** Designed a multi-host architecture to operate two independent brands with a single codebase. Achieved complete logical separation of members, consultations, and data.
*   **AWS Cost Reduction by 75% ($1,300 -> $337/mo):** Eliminated over-engineering (Kafka, Redis Cluster) and adopted a cost-effective Modular Monolith structure with local caching, tailored to actual traffic scale.
*   **Admin System Development:** Built a comprehensive Admin Dashboard (React, 40+ pages) from scratch for full operational control (User/Doctor/Pharmacy management, Order/CMS) utilizing AI tools like Claude Code.
*   **Global Service Infrastructure:** Designed i18n system (Simultaneous KR/JP support), integrated Google Meet for video consultations, and integrated local payments/delivery (Wolt, Uber Direct, SAGAWA).
*   **Dev Productivity Innovation:** Maximized build speed and maintainability by adopting Kotlin JDSL, upgrading to Spring Boot 3.x / Kotlin 2.x, and implementing Modular Monolith architecture.
